### (2026-04-13, arXiv:2604.11978) The Long-Horizon Task Mirage? Diagnosing Where and Why Agentic Systems Break

**URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11978

**URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12018

**著者**: Hamoud Alhazmi, Jiachen Jiang
